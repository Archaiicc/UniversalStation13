27 March 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Most jobs will have more access on lowpop(less than 10 players) rounds.

20 March 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Chances to miss a limb are heavily reduced. Keep in mind that targeting the chest has the least chance to miss an attack, targeting the head has the highest.
  • High gun skills improve your chances to hit a target with a projectile.
  • More guns received full-auto fire mode.
  • Assault rifles and machine gun had their magazine capacity increased.
  • Pistol and rifle bullets deal slightly less damage.

19 March 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Health HUD will no longer show "100" icon when you are at full health.

17 March 2022

ACCount12 updated:

  • Added new conveyor sprites, from CEV Eris.
  • Improved conveyor code, which allows people to use wrench on them to change direction and multitool on the switch to make it one-way.

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Dufflebags now have much less slowdown when worn.
  • Mercenary void suits now have slightly less slowdown when worn.
  • Combat belts(those can be found in ERT base) now have 0 slowdown.
  • Simple mobs can no longer randomly stun you in melee. Rejoice.
  • Random weapon stuns are now much shorter: From 20 seconds to 3 seconds and from 3 to 1.5.

Roland410 updated:

  • Foundation Advisor role has been added, their job is to oversee psionic phenomena.
  • Added a chemical reagent "Jerraman". Its main purpose is to activate psionic latencies. Foundation Advisor starts with 10 vial(4u each) of it in their office. Be careful not to overdose!
  • Counselor/Mentalist no longer has psionic powers, and the Mentalist alt-title was removed as such.
  • All crewmembers now have a low chance to start with random psionic latencies.
  • Antagonist psionic amplifier now can grant you 2 faculties instead of 1.

Spookerton updated:

  • MC tab doesn't lag the hell out of users anymore.
  • Sound player datum doesn't runtime randomly anymore.
  • Subsystems were overall updated/improved/refactored to operate faster and be less of a burden on the server.

19 February 2022

EgorDinamit, PowerfulBacon updated:

  • Added chat badges. Those appear next to the user's name in OOC chat if they possess an admin rank or a status of "trusted player".

18 February 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • You can now click on the inventory UI to retrieve the item, instead of clicking precisely on the item itself.
  • Drag&dropping an item onto itself will no longer cause it to be dropped.

01 February 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Ascent lance had its armor penetration slightly reduced.
  • Dexalin removed from the pool of chemicals available in Ascent sleepers.
  • Airlocks open and close faster.
  • GAS have much more unarmed damage when in "combat stance".

EgorDinamit, Ryll/Shaps(/tg/) for sounds. updated:

  • Field disperser now plays sound that ignores pressure. That means you will hear it regardless of it being in space.
  • Limb dismemberment now plays a sound.
  • Bashing against a window is now louder.
  • Added sounds from /tg/ for limb dismemberment.

28 January 2022

Doctor-Derp updated:

  • Rearranges the Petrov analysis lab. Adds more equipment to suit new anomaly research. Fixes Emitter wiring.
  • Adds a water tank to the rear Petrov maintenance compartment.
  • Adds cameras to the Observation room.
  • Pushes R-UST canister tanks into the wall for more space.
  • Adds a turret, turret control panel, and signage to cyborg/AI upload.
  • Adds a plunger to medbay decontamination toilets! :)
