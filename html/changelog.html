13 November 2021

AzlanOnPC, Pawn, Fre3bie, SomeAngryMiner updated:

  • EVA Hardsuit, Blood-Red Voidsuit, Crimson Hardsuit and Heavy Crimson Hardsuit have received a sprite update.
  • Mercenary EOD hardsuit is now spaceproof.
  • Vox can now wear mercenary voidsuits and hardsuits, while Skrell and unathi can now wear merc and EVA hardsuits.

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Temperature gun now only has 2 modes: heat and freeze. Both adjust the target's temperature by 40 in their respective direction.
  • Temperature gun now fires a bit faster and consumes more charge per shot.

11 November 2021

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Added fancy-looking jumpsuit. Currently unavailable outside of admin spawn.

09 November 2021

Azlan updated:

  • Updated supermatter icons.

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Players on lowpop will start with more access. Choose command positions for even more access.
  • Lowpop access is controlled by config values LOWPOP_ACCESS and LOWPOP_ACCESS_AMOUNT.
  • An abandoned tech cult church has been added to the list of exoplanet ruins.

Imienny, RustingWithYou updated:

  • Adds new emote to Vox Armalis *ashriek, *shriek but cooler.
  • The *ashriek emote sound may also play when speaking.
  • Adds two new Vox plants - the resin plant and the breather. The breather produces nitrogen gas, and the resin produces a new material, Vox bioresin. Adds seeds for both of them to the Vox offship.
  • Adds seed extractor to Vox ship.
  • Vox Armalis can now use *shriek emote.
  • Adds sprites for Vox Armalis wearing the two types of Vox clothing.
